UConn’s Geno Auriemma, Feeling Ill, Is Hospitalized - The New York Times
Geno Auriemma, the coach of Connecticut’s women’s team, has been hospitalized with an undisclosed illness. The university released a statement saying that Auriemma, 62, had boarded a plane at Bradley International Airport in Windsor Locks, Conn., but decided to get off because he was not feeling well. The university said he was taken to a hospital for observation and was resting comfortably.
