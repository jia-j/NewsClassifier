Luke Donald Grabs One-Shot Lead at Hilton Head - The New York Times
Luke Donald shot a two-under-par 69 to take a one-stroke lead on Jason Kokrak and Charley Hoffman in the RBC Heritage in Hilton Head Island, S.C., while top-ranked Jason Day, who was tied for the lead entering the round, had a season-worst 79 to fall nine shots back. ■ Minjee Lee won the Lotte Championship, closing with an eight-under-par 64 for a one-stroke victory in Kapolei, Hawaii.
