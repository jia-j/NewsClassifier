Oklahoma Men’s Gymnastics Team Wins 10th Title - The New York Times
Oklahoma successfully defended its N.C.A.A. men’s gymnastics title with a dominant performance in the finals. The Sooners scored 443.4 points to beat Stanford by slightly more than 10 points for their 10th national title. Oklahoma joined Penn State (12) and Illinois (10) as the only schools with at least 10 team championships.
