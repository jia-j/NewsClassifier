Rafael Nadal Tops Andy Murray in Monte Carlo Semifinal - The New York Times
Rafael Nadal defeated Andy Murray, 2-6, 6-4, 6-2, on Saturday to reach the finals of the Monte Carlo Masters in Monaco. If Nadal beats Gaël Monfils in the final on Sunday, he will win his ninth Monte Carlo title and will equal a record set by Novak Djokovic with his 28th Masters title. Monfils beat Jo-Wilfried Tsonga, 6-1, 6-3. ■ Simona Halep battled to a 6-4, 6-7 (4), 6-4 victory over Andrea Petkovic of Germany as host Romania evened the Fed Cup world group playoff in Cluj at 1-1. ■ Garbiñe Muguruza and Carla Suárez Navarro gave host Spain a 2-0 lead over Italy in their Fed Cup world group playoff. The fourth-ranked Muguruza beat Francesca Schiavone, 7-6 (4), 6-0, before Suárez trounced Roberta Vinci, 6-1, 6-1, on the outdoor clay court in Lleida.
