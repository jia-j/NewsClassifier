Katie Ledecky Wins Her Third Race in Three Days - The New York Times
Katie Ledecky won the 800-meter freestyle by nearly 15 seconds at the Arena Pro Swim Series meet in Mesa, Ariz. Ledecky touched in 8 minutes 13.20 seconds, for her third victory in three days. She finished 14.65 seconds ahead of Lotte Friis. Ledecky added the 800 free to her earlier victories in the 200 and 400 freestyles. She finished sixth in the 100 free.
