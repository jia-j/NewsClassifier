Creator Wins the Arkansas Derby - The New York Times
With a strong kick, Creator pulled away to win the Arkansas Derby in Hot Springs by one and a quarter lengths, giving the trainer Steve Asmussen his third victory in that $1 million race. The colt, who finished third last month in the Rebel Stakes, lagged behind the leaders for much of the race before charging ahead late.
