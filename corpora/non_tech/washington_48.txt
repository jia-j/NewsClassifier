 Redskins preseason schedule completed - The Washington Post
A day after the NFL released the regular season schedule for 2016, the league has completed the preseason schedule. The Redskins already knew the order and location of their games. But now they know the times and dates. Washington opens the preseason on Thursday, Aug. 11 at 7 p.m. at Atlanta. The team then hosts the Jets at 7:30 p.m. on Aug. 19.
